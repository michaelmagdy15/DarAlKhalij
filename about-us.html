Our Mission

Dar Al Khaleej Engineering Consultants office seeks continues and lasting improvement in its engineering professional services. As it seeks also to provide opportunities for highly qualified professionals to apply its expertise for development and community service in accordance with the highest professional standards of integrity and commitment.

Dar Al Khaleej Engineering Consultants

Dar Al Khaleej Engineering Consultants will grown into a global leader in the construction industry, creating even more value for our Client.

DEC was established in 1989 as a modest Engineering firm in Al-Ain, United Arab Emirates

Since the company inception, DEC has tremendously and rapidly expanded to launch offices in Dubai, Fujairah, Umm Al Quwain and Abu Dhabi Since the DEC has successfully completed hundreds domestic and overseas projects that include landmark worthy structure that attest to the effectiveness of our Design.

The passion of DEC is imbued into every detail of buildings that are the function of our collective expertise

DEC is vigorously pursuing overseas business as well. Currently we are operating the local subsidiaries (Cairo and Damascus) in addition to competing with the most prestigious companies worldwide. These efforts were generated in joint venture with international firm.

DEC vision is to be become one of the top Engineering companies in the region. To active their vision, crating customer value will continue to be our highest priority. Simultaneously we will continue serving our customers in the same transparency that have flourished our company.

All of our effort, are devoted to our goal of growing with our customers. Every member of DEC is united to earn your trust and guarantee your success.
