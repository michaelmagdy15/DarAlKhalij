Magdy’s approach to problem solving has laid strong foundations for DEC growth and persistent effort has become the source of the company’s remarkable progress and dynamic character. His relevant project experience includes of very large and complex facilities such as: high rise buildings, commercial buildings, governmental projects and palace etc.
